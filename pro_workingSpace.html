UI/UX Design · Interaction Design · Visual Design · Online Storage

Oct. 2020

Project Description

A self-practice project exploring collaboration across teams. It builds an environment for efficient teamwork, with two pillars: file storage and communication.

Challenges

Decentralization improves safety and efficiency. Co-management and co-creation need a link, so the design connects in-file and out-file communication to bridge that gap.

Storyboard

Communication touchpoints in offline teamwork, mapped to online flows:

  • When and why to communicate
  • Who to talk to and what to say
  • How to exchange and track feedback
  • What kinds of feedback are needed
